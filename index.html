About Touch of Elegance

Founded by Sheren — built on a passion for exquisite design.

Your wedding day is more than an event — it is the first chapter of a love story written in petals, candlelight, and quiet moments of awe. My passion lies in curating exquisite atmospheres that feel like a fairytale and live in your memory forever.

Every bloom, every drape, every flickering flame is thoughtfully composed to bring your dream to life — softly, beautifully, and entirely yours.
